Blogs

Understanding Environmental Modeling: A Key to Sustainable Future

Environmental modeling simulates natural processes like floods and climate change. It helps with DRR, resource management, and planning. Types include hydrological models and flood hazard mapping.

Basics of HEC-RAS 1D and 2D Modeling: Tools for Flood Risk Assessment

HEC-RAS models water flow. 1D is for river channels; 2D adds floodplain depth. Together, they give realistic flood hazard predictions for disaster management.

GIS Applications in Environmental Science: Mapping Our World

GIS helps analyze hazards, land use, and resources. It supports mapping, urban growth monitoring, and effective environmental decision-making.
